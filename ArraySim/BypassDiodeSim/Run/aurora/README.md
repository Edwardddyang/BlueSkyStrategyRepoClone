# Aurora (Gen 12) Array Simulation

Bypass diodes used in string 1 (front of the car) are https://www.ti.com/lit/ds/symlink/sm74611.pdf?ts=1750740024367&ref_url=https%253A%252F%252Fwww.google.com%252F with a forward voltage of 0.026 V and a resistance of 0.00325 ohms.

Bypass diodes used in string 2 (back of the car) are https://www.digikey.com/en/htmldatasheets/production/777064/0/0/1/spv1001 with a forward voltage of 0.07 V and a resistance of 0.00875 ohms.

Array cells are the Maxeon SunPower Gen7 PE with an open circuit voltage of 0.739V, short circuit current of 6.415 amps, Vmp of 0.647 V and Imp = 6.06 V https://xxrsolarpv.com/product/en/Sunpower-Maxeon-Gen7-half-cut.html (not exact datasheet)
